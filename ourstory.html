Our Story

Flash back to nearly six years ago, when both Amit and Amanda were attending graduate school in Philadelphia.


In April 2011, Amit’s childhood friend, Taky, came to visit Amit and suggested that they meet up with a friend of his from college who also happened to live in Philly. That friend was Amanda, and she invited Taky and Amit to join her and some of her law school friends at a nearby bar, where they were celebrating the completion of oral arguments. After chatting over margaritas and music bingo at Landmark, the boys called it a night, but not before Taky suggested that Amit and Amanda stay friends. Amanda agreed and suggested that she and Amit exchange phone numbers. They stayed in contact, went on their first date later that week, and have been enjoying each other’s company ever since.
